Taste of Tunisia Menu

Appetizers

  • Brik

    Phyllo pastry filled with egg, tuna, parsley, and capers, fried until crispy.

  • Hmiss (Harissa)

    Spicy chili paste made from roasted peppers, garlic, and various spices.

  • Merguez Sausage

    Spicy lamb sausage flavored with harissa and other Tunisian spices, grilled and served with bread.

Main Courses

  • Caprese Salad

    Fresh tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil drizzled with balsamic glaze.

  • Couscous with Lamb

    Steamed couscous topped with tender lamb stewed with vegetables and chickpeas.

  • Tagine with Fish

    Slow-cooked fish stew with tomatoes, potatoes, olives, and preserved lemons, flavored with Tunisian spices.

Desserts

  • Chocolate Fondue

    Assorted fruits and marshmallows served with rich chocolate fondue.

  • Makroud

    Deep-fried semolina cookies filled with dates and flavored with orange blossom water.

  • Bambalouni

    Deep-fried Tunisian donut made from yeast dough, sometimes filled with dates or flavored with orange zest.
